# HyperFocus Marketing Strategy

**Document:** MARKETING.md  
**Product:** HyperFocus (ADHD Planner)  

## 1. Viability Classification
**Tier 1: Cash Cow**  
High impulse-buy demographic, heavily active on social media, actively seeking alternatives to expensive subscriptions.

## 2. Target Audience & Ad Platforms
- **Primary Platform:** TikTok & Instagram Reels (UGC focused).
- **Targeting:** Broad algorithmic targeting using hashtags: #ADHD, #ExecutiveDysfunction, #ADHDParalysis.

## 3. Core Marketing Hook (Ad Copy)
"Are you paralyzed by a messy room? This anti-planner hides your to-do list and gives you ONE absurdly simple step. No $50/mo coaching. No ADHD Tax. Paid once, yours forever."

## 4. Geographic & Pricing Strategy
- **Global Launch:** English only initially. Maintain the $14.99 price point globally to position it explicitly against expensive $50/month subscription coaches.
